Odin Recipes

A short collection of delicous family recipes for you to try at home!

Lasagna

A hearty Italian-American favorite layered with tender pasta sheets, rich meat sauce (or savory marinara), creamy ricotta cheese filling, and gooey melted mozzarella. Baked until bubbly and golden, this comforting casserole is perfect for family dinners, gatherings, or meal prep. One bite delivers the perfect balance of savory, cheesy, and saucy goodness in every slice.

Meatoballo

Savory, tender meatballs packed with garlic, Italian herbs, and grated cheese. Simmered in tangy marinara or baked golden — the ultimate addition to pasta, sandwiches, or party platters.

Spaget

A rich, slow-simmered Italian meat sauce (ragù) made with ground beef, onions, carrots, celery, tomatoes, garlic, and herbs, served over perfectly cooked spaghetti and finished with grated Parmesan cheese. This hearty, comforting classic is a family favorite for weeknights or cozy weekends.

Pizza

A crispy crust layered with rich tomato sauce, stretchy mozzarella cheese, and savory toppings. Fresh, cheesy, and endlessly customizable — the ultimate crowd-pleasing meal.
